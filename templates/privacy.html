Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your data

Data Collection

SpotScrape collects minimal data to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Username, email, and encrypted password for registered users
  • Usage Data: Spotify URLs you extract and extraction history
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, and device information for security

Data Usage

We use your data to:

  • Provide Spotify metadata extraction services
  • Maintain your extraction history and statistics
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

Data Protection

Your privacy is important to us:

  • Encryption: Passwords are hashed using SHA-256
  • No Sharing: We never sell or share your personal data
  • Local Storage: Data is stored securely on our servers
  • Access Control: Only you can access your account data

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your stored data anytime
  • Export: Download your data in JSON or CSV format
  • Delete: Clear your history or delete your account
  • Update: Modify your account information

Cookies & Tracking

SpotScrape uses minimal tracking:

  • Session Cookies: To keep you logged in
  • Theme Preference: To remember your dark/light mode choice
  • No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics or similar services
  • No Ads: No advertising cookies or trackers

Third-Party Services

We interact with external services:

  • Spotify: We extract public metadata from Spotify URLs
  • YouTube: We find YouTube videos for music previews
  • CDNs: Bootstrap and fonts are loaded from CDNs

We don't share your personal data with these services.

Data Retention

How long we keep your data:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Extraction History: Indefinitely for registered users
  • Guest Data: Cleared periodically
  • Logs: Security logs kept for 30 days
